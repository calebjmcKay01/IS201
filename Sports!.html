About Me

For me, sports is a huge part of life. While I never was the most athletic growing up, I loved watching sports with a passion. The energy, the rivalry, the explosiveness -- It is all electrifying for me. I particularly love BYU. My siblings, parents, and grandparents have all gone to BYU; it flows in my blood. As a fan, I enjoy sifting through rankings and statistics to see if I can predict what the outcomes will be for different games I may be biased in my placement of BYU, but my overall knowledge of college football is significant enough to have my own opinions on where all teams should be ranked. I hope you enjoy Sports According to Caleb!
